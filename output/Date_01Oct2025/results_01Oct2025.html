CIRCOR International Completes Acquisition of Swelore Engineering and Hiro Nisha Systems
BURLINGTON, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CIRCOR International, Inc., a KKR portfolio company and global manufacturer of flow control products for industrial and aerospace markets, today announced that it has completed the previously announced acquisition of Swelore Engineering Pvt. Ltd. and Hiro Nisha Systems Pvt. Ltd., two leading Indian manufacturers of metering and reciprocating pumps.

ZenaTech Strengthens Drone as a Service Capabilities for Government and Aviation Markets Closing Acquisition of Smith Surveying Group in Jacksonville, Fla.
VANCOUVER, British Columbia, Dec. 02, 2025 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- ZenaTech, Inc. (Nasdaq: ZENA) (FSE: 49Q) (BMV: ZENA) ("ZenaTech"), a technology business solution provider specializing in AI (Artificial Intelligence) drone, Drone as a Service (DaaS), enterprise SaaS, and Quantum Computing solutions, announces the completion of its acquisition of Smith Surveying Group LLC, a Jacksonville, Fla.-based land surveying and inspection firm with longstanding expertise and clients across municipal, aviation, and commercial markets. This 13th acquisition added to the Drone as a Service network strengthens the companys capabilities to serve government and aviation markets in one of Floridas fastest-growing metropolitan areas also known for its aviation and aerospace presence.
